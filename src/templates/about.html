About Universal Floral

The office plants we install and maintain are a reflection of us as much as they are of our clients. That’s why we take extra special care during each phase of the interior landscaping process. Before we install the first plant, our designers visit your facility to check all factors that may create problems such as lighting, heating, cooling, traffic flow and interior design. Then we develop a unique individual plan using plants that will complement your facility and flourish in it. We can work with your design team or directly with you. Universal Floral Office Plant Rental team are here to develop the best possible floral package for your business. Call us today on 353 1 269 5216 for a free quote

We attribute a great deal of our success to four main factors:

  • High-Quality Plant Installations.
  • Professional Design Service geared to the best possible use of plants and foliage within a particular area.
  • Computer Scheduled Back-up Service to keep installations in pristine condition at all times.
  • Realistic, extremely competitive rental and maintenance price structures.
