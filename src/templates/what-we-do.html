What we do

Universal Floral Office Plant Rental & Maintenance is a family company specialising in interior landscaping for over 40 years.

Traditionally, employers have viewed plants as the finishing touch to their buildings. But as more and more research has shown that plants reduce stress, improve concentration and increase productivity, enlightened companies understand the very real benefits plants can have in the workplace.

Universal Floral offer the following services:

  • Office Plant Rental
  • Office Plant Maintenance
  • Pots & Planters
  • Corporate Floral Displays
  • Bespoke Interior & Exterior Landscaping
